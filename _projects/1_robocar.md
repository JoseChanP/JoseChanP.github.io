### The Goal
Building a 1/10th scale autonomous vehicle capable of lane following and obstacle avoidance.

### Hardware & Software
* **Compute:** Raspberry Pi 5 with OAK-D Lite camera.
* **Control:** VESC motor controller integration via ROS2.
* **Vision:** Custom lane detection pipeline.
